Stop memorizing and start creating

Posted on November 30, 2021

You must not use your brain for retaining information that can be easily access thought a notepad.

You got a nice idea for your business? Go and write that down.

So you need to rembember to wash your shoes for your next date. Cool. Write that shit down.

Got it, right? The thing is we need to use our brain to critical and creative thinking not remembiring stuff. This takes more relevance now with the fancy productivite apps but we have pencils and papers since centuries. So it's not really something new.

Which it's new is becoming less usufull to know everything. I'm a software engineer and I spend a big part of my day just googling.

And this applies to almost every job. In the past to become a driver it was a requisite to know every corner of the city. Now you can just download Uber and use the GPS. Of course it's important to have an understading of the city because you can easily get lost otherwise.

But the main thing of all of this is: Stop triying to memorize and focus to undestanding and adapting.
